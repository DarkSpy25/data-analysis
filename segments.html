Analysis of SIAM segments

As per SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) classification of segment is done on the basis of the length and body type of the vehicle. These details are taken from Team-BHP.

  • A Segment - Small/mini hatchbacks. Ex - Renault Kwid, Maruti S-Presso/Alto 800
  • B1 Segment - Regular hatchbacks. eg. Maruti Swift/WagonR, Hyundai Grand i10 Nios, Tata Tiago etc.
  • B2 Segment - Premium hatchbacks and compact SUVs. eg. Hyundai i20, Maruti Baleno, etc.
  • C1 Segment - Entry level sedans and larger SUVs. eg. KIA Sonet, Hyundai Venue, Tata Nexon, etc.
  • C2 Segment - Proper sedans and also includes the bigger/premium SUVs. eg. Honda City, Maruti Ciaz, Skoda Slavia/Kushaq, etc.
  • D1 Segment - Larger and more powerful sedans and the larger SUVs. eg. Skoda Octavia, Honda Civic,Jeep Compass, etc.
  • D2 Segment - Premium sedans and SUVs eg. Honda Accord, Toyota Camry/Fortuner, Ford Endeavor
  • Premium - Luxury brands and their Sedans and SUVs. eg.s Audi, Lexus, BMW, Porsche, Jaguar etc.
  • Utility - Big rugged 4X4's. eg. Mahindra Thar, Force Gurkha, Toyota Hilux, Isuzu D-Max etc.

Using ANOVA, we can see that the segments as classified by SIAM vary significantly from one another.


Segmentation as per K-means Clustering

Divided the dataset into 7 clusters using the K-means algorithm in unsupervised learning. This was done using the BigML extension in Google Sheets.

  • Removed premium and utility cars while clustering because there are a wide range of cars in those segments. The clustering wasn't very effective due to this.
  • Considered showroon price, displacement and volume for clustering as they produced the most accurate clusters.

Comparison of SIAM segments with K-means Clustering

The K-means clustering algorithm provided better results while grouping cars which have models spread across a wide price range. For example the Tata Nexon has models from 7 lakh to 12 lakh. In the SIAM categorization all these cars come under the C1 segment.

But in the K-means clustering they are divided into 2 segments. Thus, it is a more accurate representation of the dataset.
